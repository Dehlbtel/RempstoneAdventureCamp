Membership Application and Booking Form 2016

Please fill in the following information and then press the "Check Application" button. A unique reference number will be created which will then be used for all correspondence. Once you are certain all the information is correct then check the confirmation boxes and press the "Apply Now" button (Note: please check all the information very carefully as it is not possible to correct it on-line once the "Apply Now" button has been pressed). Your application will not be confirmed until the correct payment has been received.

Please Note: This form requires javascript to perform the processing and data upload. Please ensure that you have enabled javacript in your browser; if nothing appears to happen when you press the "Check Application" button then there is a problem with running the javascript code.


If you have any problems with this form (or need to correct anything after submission) then please email admin@rempstoneadventurecamp.org.uk

* Required Information



Camper Details

  

Members' email addresses are not given to anybody other than those directly involved in organising the camp. Copies of any information emailed to members under 16 are sent to parents if an email address is provided.

If you are currently in year 11 then you can choose whether you want to come as a camper or would prefer to be considered as a Junior Leader; if you are in year 12 or higher you must do this. To come as a Junior Leader you will need to discuss this with Docco (Revd Dr Jonathan Holmes). Please submit this application form first and select "Junior Leader" in the "School Year" dropdown if you wish to take this route.

If you will be 18 or over at the start of camp then you can be invited to come as a Leader. You will need to submit this application form and you should then receive the "Leader admin pack" and instructions on the rest of the application and approval process.

If you would like to serve as a member of the cook team then you will need to submit this form and you should then receive the team-cuisine application pack.

Payment Details


This is how you intend to pay. Full details will be emailed when this application is processed.

Rempstone is only able to operate competitively through the support of those volunteering to be part of the team that organises and delivers the camp, both in terms of time contributed as well as financially. We would normally ask that you consider making a donation of the most appropriate amounts from the choices below. We appreciate that many of our volunteers may need to vary this amount, for reasons that may include not being available for the whole of camp, or current financial circumstances, or agree to make smaller, regular payments over a number of months. If this is the case, please select the last option below and contact one of the finance team in confidence, either personally or at finance@rempstoneadventurecamp.org.uk. It is important that this happens promptly, and certainly before final payment is expected, so that we can discuss as necessary, update our records, and budget accordingly.
