2014 T Shirts and Hooded Tops

Included in camp membership is an exclusive 2014-design Rempstone t-shirt, chosen from the colours and styles below. If you prefer, you can choose one or more of the other items instead of (or as well as) the t-shirt - the first item will be discounted in lieu of the t-shirt.

Please complete the form below indicating your choice(s) of item, size and colour. Even if you are not choosing additional items, please choose a style, size and colour for your inclusive t-shirt unless you have decided you do not want to receive one (select the relevant option from the first "item" box).
